About Developer

Over the past decade, the Prestige Group has solidified its position as one of India’s leading and most successful real estate developers, leaving a significant impact across various asset classes. Established in 1986, the company’s remarkable growth has been driven by CMD Irfan Razack and his brothers, Rezwan Razack and Noaman Razack. Over the years, Prestige Group has diversified into a range of related and unrelated services, each led by highly skilled individuals. Their ventures include interior design through Morph Design Company (MDC) and a sophisticated take on men’s formal fashion with Prestige Fashions (P) Ltd. Prestige Group stands out as the only developer in South India with such a diverse portfolio, spanning residential, commercial, retail, leisure, and hospitality segments.
